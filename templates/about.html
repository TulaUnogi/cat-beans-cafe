About Us

Welcome to Cat Beans Café, the place for all coffee and cat lovers!
We're a small café with a big mission. Our team consists of friendly, full of positive energy visionaires. Our combined passion to coffee, baking and cats resulted in opening the door of Cat Beans Café in 2023.
We're working in co-operation with Cat and Dog Protection Association of Ireland to help find new homes for our kitties while providing the best cat café experience to our both human and feline friends.
You can be sure that visiting us is going to make you purrrfectly cosy!
